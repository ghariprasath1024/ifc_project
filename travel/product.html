OOTY

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station in Tamil Nadu, India, renowned for its scenic beauty, lush tea gardens, and pleasant climate. It's often called the "Queen of the Hills". Key attractions include the Government Botanical Garden, Ooty Lake, Doddabetta Peak, and various tea estates. The town is also known for its colonial-era charm and is a favorite destination for tourists seeking natural beauty and a peaceful escape.
